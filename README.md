# 📊 Employee Attrition — Power BI Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard visualising employee attrition patterns
across departments, age groups, income levels and overtime status.
Built as part of my Data Science learning journey.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This dashboard extends the Employee Attrition ML project by presenting
the key findings in an interactive business intelligence format. It allows
HR managers and executives to explore attrition patterns across different
employee segments without any technical knowledge.

---

## 📊 Dashboard Components

**Visuals:**
- Attrition Rate by Department — Sales has the highest attrition
- Attrition Rate by Age Group — Teens and Young Adults churn most
- Attrition Rate by Overtime — overtime employees leave at 3x the rate
- Attrition Rate by Monthly Income — lower income drives higher attrition

**Slicers:**
- Gender
- Department
- Job Role
- Business Travel

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Sales department has the highest attrition rate at ~20%
- Employees aged 18-30 are significantly more likely to leave
- Overtime is the strongest visual predictor of attrition
- Lower income employees show consistently higher attrition rates
